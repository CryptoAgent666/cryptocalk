## 3. Full Description — English (max 4000 chars)

CryptoCalk is a free, privacy-focused cryptocurrency calculator suite covering every stage of the crypto workflow — from evaluating a trade before you enter it, to estimating the tax bill after you exit.

There are no ads, no account required, no personal data collected, and no paywalls. Every calculation runs locally on your device. Open the app, get your number, close the app. That is the entire experience.
